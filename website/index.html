SoccerBot

EECS 106A Final Project, Fall 2021

Introduction

The project was divided into two tasks: Computer Vision and Trajectory Optimization.
As per the project guidelines, our work involved sensing, planning, and actuation. Sensing and Planning was done through the computer vision, where the location of the ball was found. After calculating the location of the ball relative to the robot, the desired kicking motion was planned. Actuation was be carried out through the robot itself, and we utilized the low-level controls of the Unitree Robots Repository to control and map motion of the A1 robot.
